## Family Day established as public holiday


**1** The day in February prescribed under this Act is a public holiday to be observed as "Family Day".


## Day set by regulation


**2** The Lieutenant Governor in Council may by regulation prescribe a day in February to be observed as "Family Day".


## Commencement


**5** This Act comes into force on the date of Royal Assent.
